Summary Bio

Ricardo is a a native of Uruguay, has spent thirteen years in the U.S. and moved to Minnesota five years ago.

With a degree in Economics, he currently works for Johns Hopkins University/Jhpiego as the Financial Reporting and Analysis Manager.

Back home, in his years as a student, he founded and managed a horseback riding program across the Uruguayan grasslands for international riders. Through that experience he promoted friendship, cultural understanding and opened the doors of the Uruguayan countryside to foreign visitors.
